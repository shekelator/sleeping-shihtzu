Drash notes

Here are some notes we discussed together. Copy these into a doc and start writing out some ideas or points you'd like to make, maybe with supporting quotes from the texts. See the links above, or your booklet, to read the texts.

  • What does it mean to offer something from the flock or herd?
  • What does it symbolize? (1:1-2)
  • Why should the burnt offering be a male? Why without blemish?
  • Why must a person bring the offering him or herself?
